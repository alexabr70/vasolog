AppGallery Review Notes - VasoLog v1.1.1 (versionCode 3)
==========================================================

Dear Reviewer,

This is a resubmission of VasoLog. We have addressed the issues from the
previous review. Please find below the list of changes corresponding to
each rejection reason.

1. Rule 7.1 - Privacy Policy consent pop-up on first launch
-----------------------------------------------------------
Added: A blocking Privacy Policy consent dialog is now shown on the very
first launch of the app, BEFORE the onboarding screens. The dialog:

- Cannot be dismissed by tapping outside or pressing back (PopScope).
- Displays the developer name (Aliaksandr Abrashkin), the full summary
  of what data is collected, where it is stored, and a clickable link
  to the full Privacy Policy (https://alexabr70.github.io/vasolog/privacy_policy.html).
- Has two buttons: "Agree and continue" (proceeds into the app) and
  "Decline and exit" (closes the app via SystemNavigator.pop()).
- The consent flag is stored in SharedPreferences (pp_accepted_v1) so
  the dialog is shown only on first launch.

Implementation:
- lib/main.dart: _AppRoot + _PrivacyConsentScreen widget
- Dialog is rendered before OnboardingScreen / MainShell.

How to reproduce: Install fresh -> on first launch, the popup appears
before any other screen.

2. Rule 7.5 - Developer name in Privacy Policy
-----------------------------------------------
The Privacy Policy now explicitly states the developer name in three
places:

a) Online HTML Privacy Policy (the URL shown in AppGallery listing):
   https://alexabr70.github.io/vasolog/privacy_policy.html
   - Overview paragraph: "... developed by Aliaksandr Abrashkin
     (individual developer)."
   - Contact section: "Developer: Aliaksandr Abrashkin (individual
     developer). ..."

b) In-app About screen (Settings -> About):
   The Privacy Policy section now starts with
   "Developer: Aliaksandr Abrashkin"
   followed by the summary text.

c) Privacy Policy consent dialog on first launch (see #1):
   Shows "Developer: Aliaksandr Abrashkin" in bold above the summary.

3. Chinese mainland removed from distribution regions
