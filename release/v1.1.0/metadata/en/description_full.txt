Cold hands, color changes, numbness — Raynaud's phenomenon turns ordinary moments into painful interruptions. Now you have a tool built specifically for your condition.

VasoLog is a medical-grade tracker for Raynaud's phenomenon that helps you understand your episodes, identify what triggers them, and communicate effectively with your doctor.

── TRACK EVERY EPISODE ──
Log each attack in seconds: which fingers are affected, the color phase (white ischemia → blue cyanosis → red reperfusion), severity on the validated RCS scale (0–10), and duration. Add a photo and personal notes for complete documentation.

── AUTOMATIC WEATHER DATA ──
Every episode is automatically paired with real-time weather conditions: temperature, humidity, atmospheric pressure, and wind speed — captured at your exact location. No manual entry needed.

── IDENTIFY YOUR TRIGGERS ──
Select from common triggers: cold exposure, emotional stress, vibration, smoking, caffeine, and more. Over time, VasoLog reveals patterns — showing you exactly what provokes your episodes.

── CHARTS & INSIGHTS ──
The dashboard displays your episode frequency over the last 7 days and your monthly trigger breakdown. Spot patterns, track your progress, and understand how seasons affect your condition.

── PDF REPORT FOR YOUR DOCTOR ──
Generate a professional medical report covering any date range with a single tap. Share it instantly with your rheumatologist, vascular specialist, or GP — saving precious consultation time and helping your doctor make better decisions.

── YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS ──
All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no account, no tracking. Complete privacy — your health information belongs to you alone.

── HOME SCREEN WIDGET ──
Keep your last episode summary visible on your home screen for quick reference and reminders.
